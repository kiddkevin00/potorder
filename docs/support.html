Support

Frequently asked, honestly answered.

If your question isn't here, open an issue on GitHub. Public, traceable, and the same channel I use for everything else.

How fast does the belt get?

Belt travel time scales from 6 seconds down to 2.2 seconds based on your current score (linear ramp ending around score 60). Spawn rate also tightens.

What exactly triggers Fever?

Hitting a combo of exactly 8 (and every multiple of 8 thereafter, if not already in Fever). Fever lasts six seconds and doubles every point during that window.

Why do I lose a life when an item slides off?

An item that slides off without being tapped counts as a miss only if it matched the active order. Items of other colors sliding off don't penalize you.

Can I turn off haptics?

Yes — the toggle is on the start menu below the Start cooking button. The setting persists across launches.

Is there a leaderboard?

No. Best score is local to this device. There's no server to upload to and no account to sign into.

Get in touch

Need help, or want to report a bug?

Open an issue on GitHub. There's no support email — issues are the canonical channel and they're public, which keeps everything honest.
