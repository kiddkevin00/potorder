Support

Frequently asked, honestly answered.

If your question isn't here, open an issue on GitHub. Public, traceable, and the same channel I use for everything else.

Is there an undo or pause?

No undo (the conveyor never sits still). The app naturally pauses when backgrounded; the run resumes correctly on foreground.

Can I turn off haptics?

Yes — toggle on the start screen below the Start button. Persists across launches.

Why emoji instead of illustrated food?

Emoji ship with iOS, render crisply at every size, and avoid licensing custom artwork in this MVP. A future version may swap in custom illustrations; the mechanics will stay the same.

Will there be levels?

Not in this build. The single endless mode is the entire game. Levels and themed worlds are a separate scope.

Get in touch

Need help, or want to report a bug?
