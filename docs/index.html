Features

What's in the build

The whole feature set, plainly listed — no upsells gating the basics.

◉

One core loop

Pot orders an ingredient → tap the matching item on the belt → score. Repeat until three misses end the round.

×

Combo multiplier

Consecutive correct matches build a multiplier (×2, ×3, …) that scales your points up.

✦

Fever mode

Hit an 8-combo and every match doubles for six seconds. Chain another 8 to refresh it.

⏵

Belt speeds up

As your score climbs, the conveyor travel time scales down. Late game is genuinely tight.

◷

High score saved

Your best run is persisted locally. No leaderboard server, no friend compare.

⊘

No banners

No interstitials. No 'watch ad to continue'. The 25-line credits are at the bottom of the source on GitHub.

How to play

From cold-start to good-at-it

A few short steps. The app gets out of your way after that.

01

Watch the pot

The pot at the top shows one ingredient at a time — that's the active order.

02

Watch the belt

Items ride the conveyor across the bottom. They slide off the left edge on a timer.

03

Tap matching items

Tap the ingredient that matches the order. Tap wrong and you lose a life; tap right and combo bumps.

04

Hit combo 8 for Fever

Eight matches in a row triggers Fever — six seconds of double scoring. Chain another 8 to refresh.
